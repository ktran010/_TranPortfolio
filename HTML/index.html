Hey there! I'm Katherine, though I also go by Kat and sometimes even KitKat!~

My pronouns are she/her, and I'm a member of the LGBTQ+ community. I consider myself an empathetic and expressive person who, tragically, talks way too much with their hands.

I'm currently an undergrad at the University of Colorado Boulder, wherein I'm pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Creative Technology and Design and minoring in Computer Science.

I'm really interested in exploring the usage of programming and software to create artwork. Generative Art, the process of creating forms with algorithms, comes to mind. I'm also fascinated with 3D modeling and artwork, so I really look forward to learning more about these topics!

Thank you for taking the time to check out my work, I really appreciate it. Please reach out to me, I'd love to connect!
