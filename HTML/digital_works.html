C++ TERMINAL GAME


With my working knowledge of object-oriented programming, I developed a dating simulator that runs in terminal. The game begins with the player's input of their name and description of themself that they can later view in their profile. The player has the option to interact with four different characters I've created, each with their own unique personality and story. Depending on the player's choices, they can increase their affection level with the characters they interact with. The game concludes with the user's decision to send a letter to the person of their choice, and obtain a specific ending based on their affection levels.
